# ET-AI-HACKATHON-Project
AI for Industrial EV Supply Chain &amp; Asset Intelligence: Accelerating Net Zero
EVision AI

Overview

EVision AI is an AI-powered platform that helps commercial and industrial fleet operators transition to electric vehicles through data-driven insights and intelligent recommendations.

What EVision AI Does
- Analyzes fleet data such as vehicle age, mileage, fuel efficiency, utilization, and operating costs.
- Identifies vehicles that are best suited for electrification.
- Estimates annual cost savings, ROI, and payback period.
- Calculates potential carbon emission reductions.
- Provides battery performance and ownership cost insights.

Key Features
Fleet EV Switch Advisor
- EV replacement recommendations.
- Cost-benefit analysis.
- ROI and payback calculations.

Battery Health & ROI Analytics
- Battery performance monitoring.
- Degradation prediction.
- Lifetime cost estimation.

EV Marketplace
- Promotion platform for EV manufacturers.
- Visibility for charging infrastructure providers.
- Connection between fleet operators and EV ecosystem partners.

Impact
- Reduce fleet operating costs.
- Accelerate EV adoption.
- Support sustainability and ESG goals.
- Enable data-driven decision making.
- Lower carbon emissions.

Business Model
- Subscription plans for fleet analytics and AI recommendations.
- Premium reporting and enterprise solutions.
- Paid promotional listings for EV manufacturers and charging providers.
- Consulting services for fleet electrification strategies.
